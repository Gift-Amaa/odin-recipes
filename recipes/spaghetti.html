How to make Spagetti

Below is the full spagetti recipe

Here is a delicious spaghetti Jollof, one of the most popular foods in Nigeria.

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Tomato paste
  • Cooking vegetable oil
  • Onion,Pepper, fresh Tomatoes
  • Ground Crayfish (3 spoons)
  • seasoning cubes, Salt to taste
    •

    Steps To Prepare Spaghetti

    1. Cut/chop the carrot, green beans, and green pepper to desirable sizes.
    2. boil protein of your choice with all the necessary ingredients
    3. Add the sliced tomatoes/peppers and onions, fry till the water is dried, this should take about 10-15 minutes. (Remember to stir occasionally to avoid burning).
    4. Add the spaghetti and cook for about 15-20 minutes till it is almost soft then add the remaining onions, then the carrot and green beans.
